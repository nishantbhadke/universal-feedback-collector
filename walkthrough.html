Dynamic Star Rating UX & Star Contrast

Doubled star contrast to extreme high visibility (solid filled amber stars in hover/selected state and deep gray in empty). Bound rating change listeners to a dynamic textual reviews description directly under the stars, showing real-time feedback (1 - Poor, 2 - Fair, 3 - Average, 4 - Good, 5 - Excellent).

Notion-Grade Empty States & Condensed Toolbar

Condensed vertical filter boxes into a single-row responsive toolbar containing a brand-new dynamic Sort By dropdown (supporting Newest, Oldest, Highest Rated, and Lowest Rated). Added two distinct Notion-style empty states: Scenario A for filtered search yields, and Scenario B (complete with smooth scroll-focus submit CTA buttons) for empty channels.

WCAG AA Contrast & Consistent Visual Tokens

Standardized all select dropdowns and text inputs to a precise height (44px / h-11) and uniformized all border curves (rounded-xl for controls, rounded-2xl for cards). Upgraded input placeholder colors from dim gray-600 to high contrast gray-400 to achieve full WCAG AA compliance. Added focus highlights, button spinners, error validations, and slide-in success screens.
